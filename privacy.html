Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

What we collect

ShiftClock does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The shift times you enter are processed entirely in your browser and are never sent to any server.

Local storage

If you check "Remember my shift times," your start and end times are saved in your browser's local storage on your device only. We have no access to this data. You can clear it at any time by unchecking the box or clearing your browser's local storage.

Advertising

ShiftClock displays advertisements to support the free service. Ad networks may use cookies to serve relevant ads. Please refer to your ad network provider's privacy policy for details on how they handle data.

Analytics

We may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Cloudflare Web Analytics) to understand aggregate traffic. No personally identifiable information is collected.

Contact

Questions? Reach out via the feedback link on the main page.
