How to use the shift countdown timer

Enter your shift start and end time, then press Start countdown. The timer counts down in real time to the exact second your shift ends. Your browser tab will show the live countdown so you can minimize the window and check back anytime.

Does it work for overnight shifts?

Yes. If your shift end time is earlier in the day than your start time (for example, 10:00 PM to 6:00 AM), ShiftClock automatically handles the midnight rollover.

Will it remember my shift?

Check the "Remember my shift times" box and your start/end times will be saved locally on your device. Next time you visit, your times will be pre-filled.

Who is this for?

Anyone working a fixed shift — nurses, hospital staff, warehouse workers, factory employees, retail associates, call center agents, baristas, and more. If you have a shift end time, this tool is for you.
